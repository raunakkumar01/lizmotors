Car manufacturers may use public data on consumer preferences and market trends. This data can be gathered from a variety of sources, such as government agencies, industry reports, and online databases. By analyzing this data, manufacturers can gain insights into consumer behaviors, such as which models are selling well and which features are most popular. This information can be used to inform product development and marketing strategies to better align with consumer preferences and increase sales.
